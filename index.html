MARVEL SPIDER-MAN

Great power comes with great responsibilities

Spider-Man is one of Marvel’s most iconic superheroes, known for his agility, web-slinging abilities, and strong sense of responsibility. After being bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker gains superhuman powers and chooses to use them to protect New York City. Balancing his life as a student and a hero, Spider-Man constantly faces powerful villains while staying true to his belief that “with great power comes great responsibility.
